Program at A Glance

Intel has chosen to integrate Fitbit's Wearable Fitness and Health Monitors into the EEP. These devices offer a comprehensive view of an employee's health metrics, including activity levels, sleep quality, and heart rate. The purpose of these wearables is to empower employees with data-driven insights into their physical well-being, enabling them to make informed decisions to improve their health and by extension, their productivity and job satisfaction.

Why Fitbit?

Because your health deserves the best. Chosen for its reliability, ease of use, and deep health insights, Fitbit wearables offer a 360-degree view of your well-being. The measurable outcomes of using Fitbit wearables include improved physical health, better sleep, reduced stress levels, and enhanced overall well-being, contributing to a more productive and satisfied workforce.
